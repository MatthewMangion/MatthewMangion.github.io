Rupert Mangion was born on the 31st of March 1975. He was born in Malta in the village of Hamrun. Rupert lived in a small house with his parents Charlie and Marcella along side 3 siblings Helen, Gejtu and Rosanna. Rupert is ordinary family man, married to his wife Vincienne whom together they have three children, Matthew, Gabriel and Jacob. Altough Rupert is a hard working family man, he has still achieved greatness troughout his football career.
